The Evolution of the Philosophy

Pre-Socratic Philosophy

Early Greek philosophers moved away from mythological explanations and sought natural causes for the universe.

Medieval Philosophy

With the rise of Christianity and Islam, philosophy merged with theology, focusing on faith, reason, and morality.
